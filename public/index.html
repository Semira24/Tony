A Life to Celebrate

Anthony William Detter was born in 1967 and lived a life full of warmth, laughter, and an enduring love for the people around him. He carried himself with a quiet strength that drew others in, and left a mark on every life he touched.

To those who knew him, Anthony was more than a friend — he was a constant, a steady presence in the good times and the hard ones. His generosity, his humor, and his heart made the world a richer place.

Though he left us too soon in 2026, the memories he made live on in all of us. This page is a place to remember him, share the moments we cherish, and keep his spirit alive together.
